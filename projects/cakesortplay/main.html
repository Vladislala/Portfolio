Cake Sort

Cake Sort is a new kind of merge-sorting game. It's not match 3 puzzles, it's match 6 with fun and addictive color-sort gameplay. Not murmurous as water sort, nor twittering as bird sort, but cake sort takes you into a bakery where there are hundreds of 3D colorful cake and pie slices to sort and combine. As a cake maker, try to sort the colored slices on the glass plate until having a completely yummy cake to serve customers.
